Capabilities

Stop stitching together five vendors. GeoClear returns the full address intelligence stack in a single JSON response.

🏛
County FIPS
Full 5-digit Federal FIPS code on every address. Built from the Census Bureau's complete 3,234-county table. Required for HMDA, CRA, and healthcare data exchange.
📊
Census Tract
Tract and block group via the Census Bureau's official geocoder. Required for HMDA compliance, CRA analysis, fair lending, and demographic segmentation.
🌊
FEMA Flood Zone
Real-time NFHL flood zone designation and SFHA flag. Required for mortgage underwriting, insurance quoting, and property risk assessment.
✅
Confidence Score
0–100 per address based on placement accuracy, field completeness, and source authority. Know exactly how clean your data is before it enters your pipeline.
🔍
Fuzzy Matching
Typo-tolerant search via Levenshtein distance ranking. "Pensylvania Av" still finds "Pennsylvania Ave NW." Works with partial addresses, transposed digits, abbreviations.
⚡
Autocomplete
Sub-100ms typeahead scoped by state or ZIP. Integrates into any address input with 3 lines of JavaScript. Returns enriched results, not just strings.
📦
Bulk Verification
Verify 1,000 addresses in a single POST. Full enrichment on each. Built for CRM cleanup, list hygiene, import pipelines, and batch ETL jobs.
📍
Proximity Search
All addresses within N km of a lat/lon point. Bounding-box with configurable radius. Full enrichment including FIPS and confidence on every result.
🌐
Geographic Hierarchy
Drill state → county → city → ZIP → address. Every level returns address counts, FIPS codes, and metadata. Ideal for spatial analytics and territory mapping.

Most vendors make you pay separately for FIPS, census, and flood data. GeoClear returns everything in one response, every time.

"verified": true, "confidence": 97, "full_address":"1600 Pennsylvania Avenue NW", "city": "Washington", "county": "District of Columbia", "state": "DC", "zip_code": "20500", "fips": "11001", "timezone": "America/New_York", "residential": "non_residential", "latitude": 38.8977, "longitude": -77.0366, "placement": "Structure - Rooftop", "census_tract":"001100", "fema_flood": "X"
  • verified
    Boolean — address exists in the NAD master dataset. Hard confirmation, not a score. All tiers
  • confidence
    0–100 quality score based on placement accuracy, field completeness, source authority, and anomaly detection. All tiers
  • fips
    5-digit Federal county FIPS. Required for HMDA, CRA, healthcare data exchange, and government reporting. All tiers
  • timezone
    IANA identifier (e.g. America/Chicago) derived from county boundaries. Zero external calls. All tiers
  • residential
    Residential vs. commercial classification from NAD authority codes. Drives RDI scoring and list hygiene. All tiers
  • latitude / longitude
    Rooftop-level coordinates where available. Placement field indicates precision (Rooftop > Parcel > Street). All tiers
  • census_tract
    Tract + GEOID from Census Bureau's official geocoder. Required for HMDA compliance and demographic analysis. Pro+
  • fema_flood
    FEMA NFHL zone designation (AE, X, VE…) and SFHA flag. Required for mortgage underwriting and flood insurance. Pro+
