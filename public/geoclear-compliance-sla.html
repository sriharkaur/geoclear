This Service Level Agreement ("SLA") is entered into between GeoClear, Inc. ("Provider") and the subscriber identified in the associated order form ("Customer").

4. Data Attestation

GeoClear certifies that all address records returned by the API are sourced exclusively from authoritative government and public-sector datasets. The data pipeline includes:

  • Core address corpus — 198M+ US addresses from federal and public-sector sources, refreshed quarterly
  • Jurisdiction geometry — county and boundary data from official government sources
  • Flood zone determination — NFIP-compliant flood zone classifications from official federal flood mapping programs

No third-party data brokers or re-sold data are included. Full data provenance documentation is available to enterprise customers under NDA upon request.

5. Service Credits

If Provider fails to meet the 99.9% uptime commitment in any calendar month, Customer is eligible for service credits:

Monthly Uptime AchievedCredit
99.0% – 99.9%10% of monthly subscription fee
95.0% – 98.9%25% of monthly subscription fee
Below 95.0%50% of monthly subscription fee

Credits must be requested within 30 days of the incident via support@geoclear.io and are applied to the next billing cycle. Credits are the sole and exclusive remedy for uptime failures.

6. Exclusions

This SLA does not apply to downtime caused by: (a) Customer's own systems or third-party services; (b) scheduled maintenance windows communicated 48 hours in advance; (c) force majeure events; (d) Customer-initiated configuration changes; or (e) free-tier or trial accounts.

7. Uptime Calculation

Uptime is calculated as: (Total minutes in month − Downtime minutes) / Total minutes in month × 100. Downtime is defined as > 5 consecutive minutes of API unavailability as measured by Provider's monitoring systems (UptimeRobot, 1-minute polling).

8. Term and Termination

This SLA is co-terminus with the active Pro Compliance subscription. It automatically renews on each billing renewal date. Either party may terminate the subscription with 30 days written notice.
